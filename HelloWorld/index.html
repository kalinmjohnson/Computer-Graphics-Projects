Keyboard Controls for the Scene:
Hold the key to move the camera or light. The keys are grouped in rows.
r: Reset the camera and light
Up Arrow: Zoom In
Down Arrow: Zoom Out
Right Arrow: Rotate the Earth 90 Degrees to the Right
Left Arrow: Rotate the Earth 90 Degrees to the Left

Moving the light
1: Move the light in the negative x direction
2: Move the light in the positive x direction
3: Move the light in the negative y direction
4: Move the light in the positive y direction
5: Move the light in the negative z direction
6: Move the light in the positive z direction

Moving the Camera
t: Move the camera in the negative x direction
y: Move the camera in the positive x direction
u: Move the camera in the negative y direction
i: Move the camera in the positive y direction
o: Move the camera in the negative z direction
p: Move the camera in the positive z direction
Toggling the different graphics components
b: toggle the base texture on and off (diffuse and ambient light components of the base earth texture)
n: toggle the night texture on and off
s: toggle specular component on and off
c: toggle the clouds texture on and off
m: toggle the normal mapping on and off

Move the Direction the Camera is Looking
f: Move the direction the camera looks in the negative x direction
g: Move the direction the camera looks in the positive x direction
h: Move the direction the camera looks in the negative y direction
j: Move the direction the camera looks in the positive y direction
k: Move the direction the camera looks in the negative z direction
l: Move the direction the camera looks in the positive z direction
